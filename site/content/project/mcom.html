{{< p_title numb="02" title="Design Process" >}}
Understand & Relate
Started by empathizing with the end users to effectively understand the context and the human side of the story. Researched the current market for m-commerce solutions and real-world retail experiences.
Research & Analyze
Created user persona and user story workflows by conducting in-person interviews with subject matter experts and current users. Managed to distill and analyze critical themes to drive select functionalities.
Prototype & Iterate
Tested and iterated all ideas through interactive mobile prototypes. We also hosted multiple educational workshops on modern mobile practices and design team structures.

Conclusion

The design strategy and detailed documentation of high fidelity prototypes were well received by Macy's executive stakeholders. They commended us for taking a very vague project brief and distill it to specific action items and deliverables. We were subsequently engaged in the next stage of the project. It also inspired an array of new functionalities within the product road-map for the upcoming releases.

Learnings

We started the project by conducting numerous stakeholder interviews with our client; it helped us understand the tensions among different teams and construct a better project scope. In hindsight, we should've preplanned backup resources for a high profile project like this and avoided delays in the timeline. We should've involved Macys UX team in our early explorations and avoided repeating their tried-but-failed experiments.
